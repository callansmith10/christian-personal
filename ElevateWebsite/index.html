Our Process

Big Picture

We like to start with the big picture questions: What type of website infrastructure makes the most sense for your business? Do you want something that can be built once and left alone, or do you want to consistently update your site with new blog posts, imagery, or products?

Design

Next, we want to get a general idea of the message and energy you want to deliver to your site’s visitors. Understanding colors, shapes, and site flow can help us create a beautiful and functional design for you. All of our designs are responsive to give mobile visitors an excellent experience (it’s 2020 after all).

Development

Once we have a solid understanding of your needs and preferences, we start building. Depending on your objectives, we can develop detailed code to create a static website, or we can use a Content Management System (CMS) to develop a site with additional capabilities and accessible editing. We also optimize your site’s performance by using as little memory as reasonable to enable faster load times, which have been shown to retain traffic and improve conversion.

Search Engine Optimization

If you want to bump your website up as high as you can on Google, you’re in the right place. We utilize both the underlying code of your site and your readable content to highlight specific keywords for Google’s crawlers to evaluate. Additionally, once visitors arrive at your site, our page performance and designs are geared toward bringing them in and keeping them engaged.

Marketing

As useful as a website can be on its own, leveraging additional tools to drive site traffic and to establish yourself or your business as a reliable resource can make your presence on the web even more powerful. To help you access that power, we offer additional help to configure Google's My Business platform, integrate helpful social media accounts, and develop ad campaigns using the appropriate web channels for your purposes.

Payment Model

We believe in completely eliminating surprise costs for our clients by providing clear pricing before we begin working on your site. The free quote you receive will be specific to your needs and will include a line item for common expenses. If we encounter unexpected expenses during the project, we'll pay for them ourselves, or provide you with a dollar-for-dollar discount off of our original quote. Finally, you can forget about unexpected expenses wrecking your budget.

Our Services

Elevate Website Solutions isn’t just a web design company. Instead, we use a consulting approach to select, design, and develop solutions that meet the needs of your business. Additionally, our design and development services utilize project-based billing instead of hourly charges, meaning that you’ll pay one, flat fee for your entire project. This approach means you won’t have to worry about the project requiring more hours of work than originally expected. In fact, we go a step further by building project costs into our quote so you don’t have to worry about spending a single extra dollar.

Update and Support Services

Once your website is complete, it may be a good idea to consider ongoing care for the future. After all, if your business progresses over time, why shouldn’t your site? Our Update and Support Services give you access to help with a variety of web-related projects, such as website issue resolution, periodic site audits, media and content updates, and major interface updates. We understand that different websites may require different amounts of care, which is why we provide three support alternatives.

Continuous Support Plan

$400 a Month

Our Continuous Support Plan allows you to put your website’s maintenance and upgrades on autopilot, by engaging our team to conduct necessary updates and requested improvements up to 5 hours each month for a flat, monthly fee.

Quarterly Support Plan

$90 per Hour

A Quarterly Support Plan allows you to purchase 9 or more service hours at a heavily discounted rate, which can then be used at any time during the following three months. Depending on your needs, you can use all of the hours at once, or spread them out as needed.

General Support

$130 per Hour

If you think you’ll only need a little help keeping up with your website, you can purchase hourly support in 1-hour increments at any time. Upgrade to another plan at any time. Proceed with caution, though - breaking your website could end up costing more in the long run.

About Us

Why We Founded EWS

Hi, I’m Jesse, one of the founders of Elevate Website Solutions.

In 2018 I attempted to start a blog, but knew almost nothing about how to create a functional website, so I simply built a site using a popular online tool. Although I didn’t realize it then, there is so much more to building a compelling, high-performance website than simply the look of the page. Eventually, I shifted my focus from blogging to learning some of the core coding languages used in website development.

The more I learned, the more I realized how much I had missed in my previous efforts, as well as the sheer amount I still didn’t know.

Instead of trying to tackle everything on my own, I decided to enlist the help of two highly capable software and website developers (and good friends) to start this business. This journey, along with an analysis of the needs of small-to-medium-sized businesses, has led us to our mission:

Our Mission

To serve our clients with excellence, meeting their web-based needs through a comprehensive blend of consulting, design, development, testing, and search engine optimization (SEO), in order to help them improve their online business presence.
